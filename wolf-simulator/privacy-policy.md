# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game **Wolf Family Simulator** with package name `unimix.studio.wolf.simulator`.

## Information We Collect

We do not ask players to create an account and we do not intentionally collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location.

The game may use third-party services for advertising, mediation, analytics, crash diagnostics, and platform features. These services may collect technical data such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app activity, approximate location derived from IP address, diagnostics, and ad interaction data.

## Advertising and Mediation

The game may show ads through Google AdMob, Appodeal, and advertising partners connected through mediation. These partners may use device and ad identifiers to deliver, measure, limit, and improve ads.

Relevant third-party privacy information:

- Google Privacy Policy: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>
- Google AdMob: <https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543>
- Appodeal Privacy Policy: <https://appodeal.com/privacy-policy/>

## Children

The game is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Third-party advertising and platform providers may process data as described in their own policies and settings. Parents or guardians can contact us to request review or deletion of information where applicable.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information directly. Data may be processed by third-party service providers that help operate ads, mediation, analytics, crash reporting, app stores, and platform services.

## Data Retention

We retain only the information needed to operate and improve the game. Third-party providers may retain data according to their own privacy policies and legal requirements.

## Your Choices

You can limit ad personalization in your device settings. On Android, you can reset or delete the advertising ID from Google settings where available.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
